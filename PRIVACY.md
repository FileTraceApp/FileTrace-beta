# Privacy Policy for FileTrace

**Beta Tester Edition - Valid until FileTrace v1.0 Commercial Launch**

This policy governs your use of the FileTrace Beta. A finalized EULA and Privacy Policy will be provided upon the official commercial release of the software.

**Effective Date:** July 9, 2026

At FileTrace, we believe that your local files are your business. Our desktop application is designed with a local-first architecture. This Privacy Policy outlines the minimal data we collect to provide our software, process purchases, activate licenses, and support optional metadata features.

## 1. Data We Do Not Collect

To be completely clear, FileTrace does not collect, transmit, or store:

- the names of your files, folders, or directories,
- your media library contents, metadata, or viewing habits,
- or any searches you make via the app to The Movie Database (TMDB).

All file scanning, renaming, and metadata generation happens locally on your own machine.

## 2. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information required to process transactions, issue software licenses, and provide customer support.

**Account and Purchase Data:** When you purchase or register for FileTrace, you provide your email address. This is used solely to deliver your license key, send receipts, and provide customer support.

**License Activation Data:** When you activate FileTrace, our licensing server collects your IP address and a unique, anonymized hardware identifier, also called a machine fingerprint. This is used strictly to enforce license seat limits, such as preventing a single license from being used on dozens of unauthorized computers.

**Support Diagnostics (Voluntary):** FileTrace does not include automatic background telemetry or crash reporting. If you encounter an issue, the app includes a button to manually export support logs. These logs only contain basic application state data and error codes. You may voluntarily email these logs to us to help fix bugs. We will never extract these logs from your machine without your explicit action.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to handle specific functionality. We do not sell or share your personal data with them beyond what is necessary to perform these tasks.

**Lemon Squeezy:** Lemon Squeezy acts as the Merchant of Record. All payments, billing details, and license key generation are securely processed by Lemon Squeezy. We never see or store your credit card information. Please review the Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment data.

**The Movie Database (TMDB):** The FileTrace application makes direct API calls from your computer to TMDB to fetch movie and TV metadata, posters, and related artwork. This communication is strictly between your machine and TMDB. FileTrace does not log or store these lookup requests on our servers.

**AcoustID:** If you choose to identify music files, FileTrace may use the AcoustID API to match audio fingerprints and retrieve track metadata. FileTrace does not upload your audio files to AcoustID; only the generated fingerprint and required lookup data are sent for matching.

**Subtitles:** Automated subtitle downloading is not included in the beta or version 1 release. Existing local subtitle files can still be moved and renamed alongside matching media.

## 4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, such as under the GDPR or CCPA, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold, such as your email and license key.

To exercise these rights, or if you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at:
